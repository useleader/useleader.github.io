We next evaluate GPT-3 on the StoryCloze 2016 dataset \cite{mostafazadeh2016corpus}, which involves selecting the correct ending sentence for five-sentence long stories.  Here GPT-3 achieves 83.2\% in the zero-shot setting and 87.7\% in the few-shot setting (with $K=70$). This is still 4.1\% lower than the fine-tuned SOTA using a BERT based model \cite{li2019story} but improves over previous zero-shot results by roughly 10\%.
