Then we add a multiplier of 3x to account for the backwards pass (as computing both $\frac{\partial{params}}{\partial{loss}}$ and $\frac{\partial{acts}}{\partial{loss}}$ use a similar amount of compute as the forwards pass. We multiply this value by the total training tokens and the total parameters to yield the number of total flops used during training.
